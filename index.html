Frequently Asked Questions

General queries about the NPTEL Soft Skills curriculum.

What are the criteria to get a certificate?

Your certification eligibility is calculated using the following weight breakdown:

  • Average Assignment Score: 25% of the average of the best 8 assignments out of the total 12 assignments given in the course (encompassing quizzes, tasks, and essay submissions).
  • Exam Score: 75% of the proctored certification exam score out of 100.
  • Final Score: Average Assignment Score + Exam Score.
Mandatory Double-Clipped Pass Condition: You will be eligible for a certificate ONLY IF Average Assignment Score ≥ 10/25 AND Exam Score ≥ 30/75. If either criterion is not met, you will not receive a certificate even if your Final Score ≥ 40/100.

When is the exam, and how is the certificate delivered?

Date & Time of Exams: The proctored exam is scheduled for October 16, 2026. You can choose either the Morning session (9am to 12 noon) or the Afternoon session (2pm to 5pm) when registering.

Registration: Announcements will be made when the online form opens. Check the portal for details on cities where the exams will be held and terms you must agree to.

Delivery: Only e-certificates will be made available; hard copies will not be dispatched. The certificate includes your name, photograph, final score with breakup, NPTEL and IIT Roorkee logos, and is e-verifiable at nptel.ac.in/noc.

Is this NPTEL course completely free?

Yes, the course is completely free to enroll and learn from. However, if you want to earn an official certificate, you must register and write the proctored exam conducted in person at any of the designated exam centres. The exam is optional for a fee of Rs 1000/- (Rupees one thousand only).

Are the practice quizzes based on actual exam papers?

The practice quizzes inside our dashboard are synthesized to match the core questions, terms, and conceptual frameworks tested in the weekly NPTEL assignments.
